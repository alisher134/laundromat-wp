### Step 1: Access WordPress Admin
1. Go to `http://localhost:8080/wp-admin`
2. Log in with your credentials
3. Look for **"About Items"** in the left sidebar (with â„¹ï¸ icon)

### Step 2: View Existing Items
- Click **"About Items"**
- You'll see the current slider cards (e.g., "365 days", ">60 min", "low prices")

### Step 3: Edit an Existing Card
1. Click on the item title to edit
2. Update any fields:
   - **Title**: Main heading (e.g., "365 days")
   - **Secondary Title**: Detail line (e.g., "Open 365 days, 07:00â€“00:00")
   - **Description**: Tagline (e.g., "Laundry that fits your life...")
   - **Featured Image**: The icon graphic (in right sidebar)
3. To change the icon:
   - Click "Set featured image" in right sidebar
   - Upload new image or select from library
   - Click "Set featured image"
4. Click **"Update"** button
5. Visit homepage to see changes

### Step 4: Add a New Card
1. Click **"Add New Item"**
2. Fill in all text fields
3. Add an icon:
   - In right sidebar, click "Set featured image"
   - Upload a PNG icon (100x100px recommended)
   - Click "Set featured image"
4. Click **"Publish"**
5. Card appears on homepage immediately

### Step 5: Delete a Card
1. Hover over item in list
2. Click **"Trash"**
3. Card removed from homepage immediately

### Step 6: Reorder Cards
Cards display in date order (newest first). To reorder:
1. Edit the item
2. In the "Publish" box, click **"Edit"** next to "Published on"
3. Change the date/time
4. Click **"OK"** then **"Update"**

## ğŸ’¡ Content Tips

### Title
- Keep it short: 1-3 words
- Make it impactful: "365 days", ">60 min", "low prices"
- Use numbers when possible

### Secondary Title
- One clear benefit or detail
- Specific information: "Open 365 days, 07:00â€“00:00"
- Keep under 50 characters

### Description
- Emotional benefit, not feature
- "Laundry that fits your life" NOT "We are open every day"
- Keep under 80 characters (1-2 lines)

### Icon
- Must be a PNG image with transparent background
- Upload via "Featured Image" in right sidebar
- Recommended size: 100x100px to 200x200px
- Download from Flaticon, Icons8, or The Noun Project

## â“ Common Questions

**Q: How many cards should I have?**
A: 3-5 cards works best for the slider UX.

**Q: Can I change the order?**
A: Yes! Edit the item and change the "Published on" date. Earlier dates appear first.

**Q: What if I mess up the icon?**
A: The card will show without an icon. Just paste a working example from above.

**Q: Can I preview before publishing?**
A: Use "Save Draft" and check if the preview works. Note: drafts don't show on homepage.

**Q: Do changes appear immediately?**
A: Yes! Refresh the homepage to see updates.

**Q: Can I undo changes?**
A: Yes, click "Revisions" in the publish box to restore previous versions.

**Icon not showing?**
